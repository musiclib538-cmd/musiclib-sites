Account Deletion

We value your privacy. If you no longer wish to use MusicLib, you can request to have your account and all associated data permanently deleted.

What will be deleted?

Upon processing your request, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers (Firestore):

  • Your profile information (Name, Email, and User ID)
  • Your saved music library and preferences
  • Your personalized app settings

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

How to request deletion:

Please click the button below to send a deletion request from the email address associated with your MusicLib account. We will process your request and confirm via email within 5 working days.
